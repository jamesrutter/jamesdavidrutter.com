About

I'm a technology and digital fabrication expert who bridges the worlds of making, education, and modern organizational operations. With a Ph.D. in STEM Education and over a decade of experience in digital fabrication and technology leadership, I help organizations modernize their operations while empowering communities through technology education.

Currently serving as Technology Director at Haystack Mountain School of Crafts, I've transformed a paper-driven organization into a cloud-first operation while developing innovative programs like "Haystack Labs" - our Fab Lab Residency program that brings together artists and technologists.

My journey spans from DIY electronics and Arduino projects to developing educational curricula, from working with MIT's Fab Foundation network to building modern SaaS solutions. I've always been passionate about making and building - from my first websites on dial-up internet to today's powerful browser-based applications.
